## Internal Linking Strategy

**Cluster Architecture:**
- Each role pillar (e.g., `seo-for-product-managers`) serves as the hub for its cluster
- Supporting articles link back to their pillar and to 1-2 adjacent role pillars
- Cross-role articles link to 2-3 different role pillars, bridging clusters
- Tool articles link to role pillars where each tool is most relevant
- Industry-specific articles link to the roles most involved in that vertical

**Link Density Targets:**
- Each article: 2-3 outbound internal links (specified above)
- Each pillar article: should receive 8-12 inbound links from its cluster
- Cross-role articles bridge clusters and distribute authority between role silos

**Hub Pages:**
- `seo-responsibility-matrix` is the highest-authority hub (linked from most clusters)
- Each role landing page (`/executives.html`, `/developers.html`, etc.) should link to all articles in that cluster
- `/articles.html` serves as the flat index across all clusters
